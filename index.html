About

Detail is
the difference

Andrei Serikov is a Director of Launch & Operations and Owner's Representative with a track record of delivering hospitality projects that others couldn't — on time, on budget, and ready to perform from day one. Based in Nashville, he works with restaurant owners, investors, and hospitality groups across the US.

He took over Hearts & Flame when the buildout was 40% complete and behind schedule, delivering a full opening in 5 months. He reopened Casey's Irish Pub in under 60 days, generating $220K+ in the first three days. At Sirtaj Hotel in Beverly Hills, he redefined the entire concept scope while stabilizing operations mid-transition.

Certified in Construction Management and Cost Estimating at Columbia University, and licensed as a Real Estate Salesperson in California, Andrei brings both creative vision and hard operational discipline to every project.

Process

How we
build it
together

A structured, transparent process that keeps your project on time, on budget, and true to the original vision.

01

Discovery & Scope

Deep dive into your goals, constraints, timeline, and budget reality. We align on what success looks like before a single dollar is spent.

02

Planning & Strategy

Space programming, permit strategy, budget structuring, and milestone mapping. Every decision documented before work begins.

03

Team & Procurement

Contractor vetting, vendor selection, and bid leveling. I assemble the right team and negotiate on your behalf.

04

Build & Oversight

On-site coordination, schedule tracking, quality control, and real-time problem solving. You stay informed — not overwhelmed.

05

Closeout & Handover

Punch list execution, inspections, final sign-offs, and complete documentation. Your space delivered ready to operate.
